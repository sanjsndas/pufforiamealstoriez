🍪 Cookies Policy & Your Digital Privacy

At Pufforia Meal Stories, we believe in clear skies and honest ingredients — including the digital ones. This policy explains how we use cookies, how you can control them, and why they help serve our delicious content.

📌 Last updated: March 26, 2026 | Effective for all visitors worldwide

🍰 We use cookies to enhance your experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize recipe recommendations. By clicking “Accept All”, you consent to our use of cookies. You can manage preferences or reject non-essential ones.

📘 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit websites. They help the site remember your actions and preferences (like login details, language, or recipe filters) over a period of time, so you don’t have to keep re-entering them. At Pufforia Meal Stories, we use cookies to improve site performance, understand which recipes you love, and serve a seamless culinary experience.

⚙️ Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You are in control. You can adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies, or use our cookie consent tool (the banner above) to accept only necessary cookies. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect functionality — for example, you might need to re-enter preferences each visit.

  • Via Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to manage cookies through “Settings” → “Privacy & Security”. You can block third-party cookies or delete existing ones.
  • Opt-out of Analytics: You can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on to prevent data collection.
  • Interactive Consent: Use the buttons on our cookie banner to update consent anytime. Your preference will be stored for 12 months.

🛡️ Third-Party Cookies & Services

We may embed content from platforms like YouTube recipe videos, Pinterest pins, or Instagram widgets. These third-party services may set their own cookies. We do not control those cookies; please review the respective privacy policies:

  • Google (YouTube / Analytics)
  • Instagram / Meta
  • Pinterest

Additionally, our “Share a Recipe” feature and comment sections rely on secure session handling — all strictly monitored.

📋 Cookie Retention & Updates

We retain cookie-related information only as long as necessary for the stated purposes. Session cookies expire when you close your browser; persistent cookies remain up to 24 months unless manually removed. This policy is reviewed semiannually and will be updated if our data practices change. Significant changes will be notified via a banner or email newsletter.
